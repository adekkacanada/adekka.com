The exchange

Partnership is work in both directions. We are explicit about both sides.

What partners get

Early, and a say

  • Milestone access: working builds as they land, ahead of general availability.
  • Profile influence: your domain's operations and policies shape the application profiles others later adopt.
  • Direct line: you work with the founding team, not an account layer.
What we ask

A real problem, honestly worked

  • A genuine governed use case: real operations, real policies, real stakes.
  • A named owner on your side with time to engage.
  • Candour: tell us where it fails. That is the point of the programme.

How it works

Introduction

A working session on your use case: the consequential operations, the policies that should govern them, and who has to trust the evidence.

Scoping

A short written scope both sides sign off on: the governed operations, the policy model, and what a successful pilot proves.

Collaboration

A lightweight agreement, then the work: milestone builds, policy co-design, structured review.
